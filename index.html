Hands on real infrastructure

Sage Hero provides more than just virtual machines or containers. Our platform creates the environment for each student so you don't have to worry about servers, storage, switches, routers and anything in between.

Unrestricted access: Break it & Recover it

The best way to learn is by making mistakes. For this reason, the lab instance is yours with unrestricted access to practice on. When you crash it, you can recover it in one click. Our platform can recover each sequence separately so you don't have to start the course over each time you damage the lab infrastructure.

Real-time guidance

We don't leave you alone with the lab. Our TrainerBot (Sage) will guide you command by command throughout each lab sequence. Equipped with advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) capable of detecting and correcting common student mistakes. The Sage will help you learn quickly by offering you real-time guidance, saving you the frustration and stress students experience when running a lab.

Comprehensive material

The lab is organized in a way that introduces theoretical information bit-by-bit. Complex concepts are illustrated and all commands are referenced.

Easy to use, clean & intuitive design

Unlike other e-learning platforms, our design remains simple and instictively easy to use. Our simple design allows students to process complex concepts by decreasing distractions for easier memory retention.

Sage Hero helps you become the master.

With the collaboration of the best Linux trainers, we have built this next generation training platform to provide you accelerated learning programs. Sage Hero was created on the idea that guided hands-on labs running on real infrastructure are the best way to acquire professional skills.
