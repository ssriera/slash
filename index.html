100% hands on real infrastructure

More than just Virtual Machines or Containers, our platform instantiate for each student a real infrastructure: servers, storage, switches, routers ...etc.

Unrestricted access, Crash it, Recover it

Best way to learn is by making mistakes. For this reason, the lab instance is yours, with unrestricted access, to practice on. And when you crash it, you can recover it in one click. Our platform can recover each sequence separately so you don't have to start the course over each time you damage the lab infrastructure.

Real-time Coaching/Guidance

We don't leave you alone with the lab. Our TrainerBot will guide you command by command throughout each lab sequence. Equipped with advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) capable of detecting and correcting common student mistakes, the TrainerBot will help you learn quickly by offering you real-time guidance and avoiding you the frustration and stress students experience when running a lab.

  • Complex concepts are illustrated
  • All commands are referenced.
